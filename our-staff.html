WHO ARE WE?

We are the University of Cincinnati's League of Legends club, or UCLoL for short. We were founded in the Spring of 2013 at a small table in TUC and began as a simple facebook group. We then were approved to be an official part of Riot's Collegiate Program and have since shot off. We've held events from online inhouses, to full blown tournaments, and small scale social events and competitions. We also have competed in competitive events in both the collegiate and general LoL e-sports community. Throughout the year, we will continue to strive to represent the friendly and competitive spirit of the University of Cincinnati as best we can, both at home and abroad.

The overall goal of this group is to create a place where men and women at the University of Cincinnati can make friends, share stories, meet new people, improve their skills through competition of all kinds, and help create something bigger than any one of us. We are both a community and an organization. We are both social and competitive. We are UC League of Legends. Come be a part of us!! We have Bi-weekly meetings on Campus, generally on Mondays. Meeting Times and Locations are posted on our social media sites, so be sure to check out our facebook page and group to remain in the loop! We will always have the most recent announcements posted under the Announcements Tab on our website.
